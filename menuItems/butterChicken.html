Butter Chicken

Introducing our gourmet delight: Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, hails from the culinary capital of Delhi, India. This exquisite dish features tender chicken simmered in a luxurious, spiced tomato and butter sauce renowned for its sumptuous texture. It's a tantalizing cousin to Chicken Tikka Masala, distinguished by its rich, velvety sauce made from premium ingredients. Experience the essence of Indian culinary mastery with every savory bite!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Tomato
  • Chicken
  • Cumin
