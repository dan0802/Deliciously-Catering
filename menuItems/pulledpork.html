Pulled Pork

Discover the mouthwatering tradition of pulled pork, a quintessential American barbecue delight rooted in the heart of the Southern U.S. This savory dish features tender, wood-smoked pork shoulder, expertly shredded and blended with our signature sauce for a flavor sensation that's unmatched. Whether slow-cooked outdoors or with our convenient indoor methods, every bite of our pulled pork embodies authentic barbecue perfection.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cumin
  • BBQ Sauce
