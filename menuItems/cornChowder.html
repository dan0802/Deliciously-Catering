Corn Chowder

Experience the ultimate comfort in a bowl with our Corn Chowder, a hearty chowder soup where corn takes center stage. Our signature blend features sweet corn, aromatic onion, celery, and a luscious infusion of milk or cream and butter. For those seeking an extra touch of indulgence, we elevate this classic with tender potatoes or squash, savory salt pork, succulent seafood, or flavorful chicken. Dive into a symphony of flavors and textures that redefine the art of chowder.

  • Corn
